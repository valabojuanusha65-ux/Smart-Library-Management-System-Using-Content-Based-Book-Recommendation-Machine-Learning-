🤖 How Recommendations Work

Our system uses TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) vectorization to convert book metadata (title, author, category) into numerical vectors. Then Cosine Similarity measures how close each book is to your reading history. The more books you borrow, the smarter your recommendations become!
